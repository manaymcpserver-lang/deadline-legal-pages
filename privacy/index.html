Nonlate Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This policy explains what information Nonlate processes and how it is used.

What we process

  • Connection data for integrations you choose to connect.
  • Task and calendar metadata needed for app features (for example title, due date, source, and completion status).
  • App settings and focus preferences.
  • Ad consent and ad delivery data.
  • Diagnostic data for reliability (for example crash reports).

How we use this data

  • Sync tasks and events from connected services.
  • Show reminders and focus features.
  • Serve ads and apply privacy choices.
  • Keep the app reliable and secure.

Sharing

Data may be shared with providers you connect and service providers needed to run the app (for example hosting, ads, and consent tools). We do not sell personal data.

User controls

  • Disconnect integrations in app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data on your device.
  • Request data deletion by email.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact support@codeconductor.pro.
