How it works

Three steps. Then it fades into the background.

You don’t need a new system. Nonlate listens to the tools you already trust and speaks up at the one moment most productivity apps miss: right before you disappear into your phone.

01
Connect your sources
Pick from Notion, Google Calendar, Google Tasks, Google Classroom, Microsoft To Do, Teams, Outlook, Jira, Trello, Blackboard iCal — whichever ones hold your real work.
02
Choose your distractions
Mark the handful of apps that usually win. Social, games, video — whatever the honest answer is. Everything else stays untouched.
03
Get a quiet nudge
When you open a distraction, Nonlate pauses the moment and shows the next thing that’s actually due. One tap to continue anyway. No shame, no logs.
What you get

A gentler kind of productive.

Nonlate isn’t a task manager trying to replace your life. It’s a thin, respectful layer that makes the tools you already use feel like they’re finally on your side.

The intercept moment
A full-screen pause in front of your worst time-sinks. Shows your most urgent deadline with context — source, due time, how much is left. Tap to work on it, or tap to continue. That’s it.
Unified deadline view
Every task from every tool in one clean, honest list — grouped by what’s actually urgent, not alphabetical or by project.
Real-time sync
Silent push from Notion, Google Calendar, Jira, Trello and more. When your deadline moves, Nonlate already knows.
You stay in control
Every nudge is skippable. Nonlate doesn’t lock you out, report on you, or moralize. It respects that some days a scroll is the right call.
Built for the way you actually live
Students juggling Classroom and Blackboard. Designers living in Notion. Engineers in Jira. Parents on Google Calendar. Nonlate quietly gathers all of it — so the question stops being “what do I have to do?” and starts being “what do I want to do next?”

Privacy

Your deadlines. Not our product.

Nonlate is designed so the smallest possible amount of your life ever leaves your phone. No analytics dashboards on your behavior. No selling. No profile building.

  • 1Minimum-needed OAuth scopes. Each integration asks only for what’s required to read your tasks and deadlines.
  • 2Tokens stay encrypted. On-device AES-256 storage. Certificate-pinned transport. Nothing sits in plaintext.
  • 3No cross-site tracking. No third-party analytics following you around the web.
  • 4One-tap disconnect. Every integration is reversible from settings. Uninstall wipes local data.
  • 5Email-based deletion. Request complete removal any time — details here.
