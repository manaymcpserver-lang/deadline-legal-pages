Nonlate Data Deletion

Last updated: April 2, 2026

You can remove your data from Nonlate using the options below.

Option 1: Remove local app data

  • Disconnect integrations from the app settings.
  • Delete/uninstall the app from your device.
  • This removes local app data stored on your device.

Option 2: Email deletion request

Send a request to support@codeconductor.pro with subject Nonlate Data Deletion Request.

Include any account/integration identifiers needed to locate your records.

Connected services

For data held by third-party services you connected (for example Notion, Google, Microsoft, Jira), you may also need to remove data directly with those providers according to their policies.
